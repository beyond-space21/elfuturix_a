About Us

“EL FUTURIX” is a national-level technical Event proudly presented by the Circuit Clusters of Bannari Amman Institute of Technology unitedly Coordinated by the Departments of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering and Biomedical Engineering in collaboration with the BIT IEEE Student Branch. ‘El-Futurix’ embodies a futuristic vision of innovation and technological advancement, representing the next era of engineering excellence. The term "El" signifies the enduring realms of Everlasting Electronics and Electrical Domains where these cornerstones of modern technology reflects the Elevated pursuit of excellence.

El-Futurix aims to light the way forward for technological progress and creativity, serving as a beacon for talented individuals across the nation to showcase their ingenuity and passion. We extend a warm invitation to all visionary minds ready to make a lasting impact on the future of technology. Join us at El-Futurix '24, where brilliance meets innovation.
